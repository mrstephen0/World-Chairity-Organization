Plans and Goals

Our main aim is to help the needy.
Ensuring the basic needs of every human being such as food,clothing, shelter, education and medical care.

Education for everyone

By providing employment education and teaching girls or boys about their rights.education is the key to success and the only way they can be freed from the shackles of poverty indefinitely.

Food for family

We respond to food shortages on the ground around the world by providing essential food parcels with staple and nutritious long-life foods that can last a family for one to six months.

Shelter for a family

Hundreds of homeless people die every year from lack of treatment, nutrition, starvation, extreme cold or hot weather. It therefore provides housing for homeless individuals and families to ensure a fairer society and safer place.
